# Anti-automation

## V2.4.1

Verify that anti-automation controls are in place to protect against excessive calls to application functions that could lead to data exfiltration, garbage-data creation, quota exhaustion, rate-limit breaches, denial-of-service, or overuse of costly resources.

Required for Level 2 and 3

### Related CAPEC™ Requirements

CAPEC™ (3.9): [125](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/125), [130](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/130), [2](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/2), [212](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/212), [215](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/215), [227](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/227), [469](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/469)

## V2.4.2

Verify that business logic flows require realistic human timing, preventing excessively rapid transaction submissions.

Required for Level 3

### Related CAPEC™ Requirements

CAPEC™ (3.9): [125](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/125), [2](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/2), [212](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/212), [227](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/227), [26](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/26), [469](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/469)
