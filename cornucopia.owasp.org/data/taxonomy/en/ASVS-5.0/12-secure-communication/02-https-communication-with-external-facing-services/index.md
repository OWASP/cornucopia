# HTTPS Communication with External Facing Services

## V12.2.1

Verify that TLS is used for all connectivity between a client and external facing, HTTP-based services, and does not fall back to insecure or unencrypted communications.

Required for Level 1, 2 and 3

CAPEC™ (3.9): [102](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/102), [117](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/117), [157](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/157), [21](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/21), [217](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/217), [220](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/220), [31](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/31), [39](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/39), [473](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/473), [593](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/593), [594](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/594), [620](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/620), [65](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/65), [89](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/89), [94](/taxonomy/capec-3.9/94)

## V12.2.2

Verify that external facing services use publicly trusted TLS certificates.

Required for Level 1, 2 and 3
